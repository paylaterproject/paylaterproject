What is Pay Later Project (PLP)?

PLP is a decentralized buy now pay later project.

PLP is a DEFI (Decentralized Finance) protocol that is the first BNPL (Buy Now Pay Later) platform built integrating blockchain technology with its own cryptocurrency.

PLP will offer significant cost savings to all participants in the ecosystem by utilising smart contract technology and blockchains. PLP aims to be a key participant offering DEFI e-commerce and in store solutions to pers and merchants.

PLP will also allow users to pay for their shopping with any approved cryptocurrency that they hold in their PLP wallet. Shoppers will be rewarded through a program that is built on allowing all contributors to the ecosystem to benefit.
PLP is powered by the PLP token which will be used as a mechanism through which the protocol is governed. A broad distribution across the entire ecosystem including users, merchants and marketplaces will ensure that all participation is rewarded.

PLP users pay no interest and no late fees ever.

About Pay Later Project

Founded in 2021 in Sydney, Australia, Pay Later Project is a unique first of its kind platform that incorporates three emerging markets:
Buy Now Pay Later;
Cryptocurrency and Fiat currency; and
Blockchain and smart contract technology.
Pay Later Projectâ€™s is a global decentralized platform creating payment solutions for shoppers, merchants, and marketplaces that empowers customers to access the things they want and need, while still allowing them to maintain financial wellness and control with the aim of helping people to spend responsibly.
Pay Later Project users have the ability to buy now pay later without the risk of being charged interest or late fees by splitting repayments in four over a period of three months, for both online and in-store purchases. While the repayment remains outstanding the user is charged a $6 account keeping and administration fee per month by Pay Later Project until repayment is made in full.
Pay Later Project users are rewarded for doing the right thing and completing their repayments on time by receiving Pay Later Project governance tokens that can be used in various ways such as buying into a higher credit limit, used as currency to purchase products and services from Pay Later Project merchants in the Pay Later Project marketplace and able to vote on certain outcomes that can shape the future of the Pay Later Project platform. Pay Later Project users can shop and pay for products and services from our merchant partners and in our affiliated marketplaces using either cryptocurrency or Fiat currency. Users also have the ability to stake their stable coins such as Bitcoin with Pay Later Project and earn a return on the amount staked.
Pay Later Project uses blockchain and smart contract technology to harness digital currencies creating ground breaking eCommerce solutions and experiences that are appealing to people of all ages.

Shop

PLP gives approved users the ability to purchase online or in store from merchants on our platform.

At the checkout users will have the option of paying with either the PLP BNPL payment solution (for approved users) or they can use any of the approved crypto assets they hold in their digital wallets.

Users who use the BNPL solution repay the purchase in four equal instalments over 3 months. Repayments can be made with approved crypto coins or direct debits made from their debit or credit cards.

Once the instalments have been completed the user will receive a reward in the form of PLP tokens.

Buyers can also shop for their products with any of their approved crypto assets.

Users can use the PLP tokens earned to access higher purchasing limits or to vote on proposals that will continue to shape the direction of the protocol into the future. PLP tokens can also be used to shop in merchant stores.
